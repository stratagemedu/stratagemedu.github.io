Canadian Experience Summer Program

In order to facilitate the transition to life in Canada for our students, the Stratagem Learning team will be offering a Canadian Experience program. This program will allow students to experience and familiarize themselves with the Canadian way of life in their journey to making Canada their permanent home.

Every summer (June – August), students will travel to Canada to attend and participate in our “Canadian Experience” summer program. Students will attend English Language Training courses, tour the top universities and attend programs and tour Canadian landmarks while immersing themselves in the Canadian lifestyle. They will be able to learn about various university programs and career paths which will help them set their career and life goals.

The Canadian Experience summer program will allow students to experience various facets of Canadian culture and facilitate their transition to a life in Canada.

English Competency and English as a Second Language

In order to ensure the success of every students in the ILC High School program, students are required to have a high level of English language competency prior to enrollment.

Stratagem Learning is working with partners to deliver a comprehensive English language training program to help students raise their level of competency in English .

This English Language Training program has been successfully delivered to thousands of students in Canada and around the globe who are now attending some of the best Universities around the world.

Although this program is primarily intended for students seeking enrollment in the ILC program, we encourage every person interested in improving their English language competency to contact us for possible opportunities.

IELTS/TOEFL Testing and Preparation

Various academic institutions and even many employers around the globe require proof of English language competency from prospective students and employees. International Students are required to write and receive a minimum score on a standardized test designed to measure an individual’s proficiency in the English language.

Students often have a choice between one of the International English Language Test System (IELTS) or the Test of English as a Foreign Language (TOEFL) exams as part of their University, employment or immigration applications.

Stratagem Learning and its partners deliver a comprehensive IELTS and TOEFL training and test preparation program that we offer to our students to help ensure their success in these tests.

Immigration Services

In order to participate in our Canadian Experience program and to complete the last semester of study in Canada as required by the ILC program; every student will need to adhere to Canada’s immigration requirement.

For this reason, Stratagem Learning International has partnered with a certified Canadian immigration Consultant to facilitate this stressful process for our students and their families. Our certified Canadian Immigration consultant will work with you every step of the way to help you through the immigration process.
Our immigration consultants are also available to answer all immigration related questions and concerns. Contact us for more information.

Academic Guidance and University Recruitment

Our Grade 11 and 12 Students are paired with Academic Counsellors and Certified University recruiters to work with you to achieve your academic and career goals. They will work with you and guide you through University applications and even help you select the courses you need to meet the requirements for your program of choice.
